My Biography

Hi, I'm Sapulmo, Kent Bryan, the owner of a thriving online thrift shop. I’m passionate about curating unique, sustainable fashion and bringing one-of-a-kind items to people who love discovering new styles. Through my shop, I aim to make secondhand shopping easy and enjoyable, offering a variety of pieces that not only help the environment but also offer great value. While I’m committed to growing my business, I’m also focused on the future and all the exciting possibilities ahead. I’m constantly looking for ways to improve, expand, and pursue new opportunities that align with my passions. The journey is just beginning, and I’m excited to see where it takes me!
